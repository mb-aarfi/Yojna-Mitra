ABOUT

Our project focuses on bridging the gap for rural citizens by offering an AI-powered platform that simplifies access to government schemes. Rural individuals often struggle with scattered information, long queues, and missing documents when trying to avail themselves of government benefits. Our solution provides an easy-to-use interface for document verification, streamlines the discovery of eligible schemes, and offers personalized guidance to ensure users can make informed decisions. By making government schemes more accessible, we aim to improve the lives of underserved communities, empowering them to gain the support they need.
